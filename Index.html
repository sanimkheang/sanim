Kpop Demon Hunter

Saja Boys is a five-member fictional project boy group. They were created for the Netflix animation film KPop Demon Hunters.

Kpopwiki

Story

For generations, demons preyed upon humans to fuel the power of their tyrannical ruler, Gwi-Ma. Their reign of terror was only halted after a group of three women rose

as the Hunters and created a barrier, also known as the Honmoon, to shield the human world from demons. To weaken the barrier, a demon named Jinu proposed to Gwi-Ma

that they form a demon boy band named Saja Boys to secretly drain the fans' energy, which powered the Honmoon, in exchange for Gwi-Ma erasing his human memories.

The group, along with Gwi-Ma, was ultimately defeated by HUNTR/X at the Idol Awards.
